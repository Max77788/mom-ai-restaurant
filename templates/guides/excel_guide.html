Excel File Format Guide

MOM AI Restaurant Assistant

Welcome to the MOM AI Restaurant Assistant. To ensure a smooth upload and processing of your restaurant's menu, please follow the guidelines below for formatting your Excel file.

Excel File Format Requirements

Your Excel file must be structured with the following columns:

  • Column A: Item Name
  • Column B: Item Ingredients
  • Column C: Item Price in USD

Example

Item Name (Column A) Item Ingredients (Column B) Item Price in USD (Column C)
Margherita Pizza Tomato, Mozzarella, Basil 10.00
Caesar Salad Romaine Lettuce, Croutons, Parmesan, Caesar Dressing 8.50
Grilled Chicken Sandwich Chicken Breast, Lettuce, Tomato, Mayo, Bun 7.75

Uploading Your File

Once your Excel file is formatted correctly, you can proceed to upload it through the MOM AI Restaurant Assistant interface. The system will process your menu items and ingredients to provide valuable insights and assistance for your restaurant.
