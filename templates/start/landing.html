How to Get Started with MOM AI Restaurant Assistant

Follow these simple steps to set up your restaurant assistant:

  1. Sign Up for an Account
    1. Visit the MOM AI website and click on the "Sign Up" button.
    2. Fill out the registration form with your restaurant's details and your contact information.
    3. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox.
  2. Upload Your Restaurant Menu
    1. Log in to your account on the MOM AI platform.
    2. Navigate to the "Menu Management" section.
    3. Upload your restaurant's menu by either:
      • Dragging and dropping a file (PDF, DOC, etc.).
      • Manually entering menu items using the provided template.
    4. Review and save your menu.
  3. Customize Your Order Script
    1. Go to the "Order Script" section.
    2. Create and customize the script that the AI assistant will use to interact with your customers. Include:
      • Greeting messages.
      • Order confirmation prompts.
      • Payment instructions.
    3. Use the preview feature to see how the script will look in action.
    4. Save your script once you are satisfied with the customization.
  4. Configure Payment Settings
    1. Navigate to the "Payment Settings" section.
    2. Link your preferred payment gateway (e.g., Stripe, PayPal).
    3. Set up payment notifications to ensure you are alerted when transactions are processed.
    4. Save your payment settings.
  5. Activate Your AI Assistant
    1. Go to the "AI Assistant" section.
    2. Activate the assistant by toggling the activation switch.
    3. Test the assistant using the built-in simulator to ensure everything works smoothly.
  6. Monitor and Manage Orders
    1. Access the "Order Dashboard" to view incoming orders.
    2. The AI assistant will notify you of new orders via email or SMS after successful payment.
    3. Track order statuses and manage your restaurant operations efficiently.

Pricing Overview

  • Message Fee: $0.05 per message sent by the AI assistant.
  • Order Fee: 1% of the total price of each formed order.
  • Estimated Cost per Order: Approximately $0.20 based on average message volume.

No upfront payments required! Pay as you go based on the usage of the AI assistant. Scalable solution to handle high volumes during peak times. Real-time notifications to keep you updated on new orders.
