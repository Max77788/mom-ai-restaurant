How to Get Started with MOM AI Restaurant Assistant & What It Does

Video Guide

Follow these simple steps to set up your restaurant AI-assistant:

  1. Sign Up for an Account (3 minutes)
    1. Visit the MOM AI landing page - you are here!
    2. Fill out the registration form with your restaurant's details and your contact information.
    3. Verify your email address by entering the code sent to your inbox.
    4. Get the link to your plug-and-play account dashboard in mid-June. Stay updated here.
  2. Setup the Assistant (2 minutes) - Available in mid-June
    3. In-store
    1. Pick up the tablet
    2. Access the Dashboard after registration.
    3. Click "Assistant" tab
    4. Press "Open [your restaurant name]'s Assistant"
    5. Put the tablet at the counter of your restaurant and let the assistant handle the orders.
    Online
    1. Access the Dashboard after registration.
    2. Click "Assistant" tab
    3. Copy the link to your restaurant's AI assistant
    4. Share the link with your customers via social media, email, or your website.
  3. Monitor and Manage Orders (lifetime of profit)
    1. Access the "Order Dashboard" to view incoming orders.
    2. The AI assistant will notify you of new orders via email or SMS after successful payment.
    3. Track order statuses and manage your restaurant operations efficiently.

Pricing Overview

  • Pay as you go based on the usage of the AI assistant.
  • Message Fee: ~$0.03 per message sent by the AI assistant.
  • Order Fee: 1% of the total price of each successfully paid order.
  • Estimated Cost per Order: Approximately $0.20 based on average message volume.
  • No upfront payments required! At the start, you receive $3 to pay for chat messages and generate your first AI order!

How do I access my funds?

Once you earn $100 on the MOM AI platform, you can visit the "Withdraw Funds" section on your dashboard and request the withdrawal of your funds in a way convenient for you, including crypto!
