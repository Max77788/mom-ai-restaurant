How to Get Started with MOM AI & What It Does

Video Guide

Follow these simple steps to set up your restaurant AI-assistant:

  1. Sign Up for an Account (3 minutes)
    1. Visit the MOM AI landing page – You're already here!
    2. Fill out the registration form with your restaurant's details and your contact information.
    3. Verify your email address by entering the code sent to your inbox.
    4. Log in and get access to your plug-and-play account dashboard. Your restaurant will be automatically displayed on the dashboard of the network of AI-empowered restaurants.
  2. Set Up the Assistant (2 minutes) In-store Setup:
    1. Pick up the tablet.
    2. Access the Dashboard.
    3. Click the "Assistant" tab -> "Open Directly".
    4. Press "Open [your restaurant name]'s Assistant".
    5. Place the tablet at the counter and let the assistant handle the orders.
    Online Setup:
    1. Access the Dashboard.
    2. Click the "Assistant" tab -> "Get the link".
    3. Copy the link to your restaurant's AI assistant.
    4. Share the link with your customers via social media, email, or your website.
    5. Add the assistant window directly to your restaurant's website by going to the 'get the code' section and following the simple instructions.
  3. Monitor and Manage Orders (lifetime of profit)
    1. Access the "Order Dashboard" to view incoming orders.
    2. The MOM AI Restaurant Telegram bot will notify you about new orders via Telegram chat (works in the background).
    3. Track the orders' details & manage your restaurant operations efficiently.

Pricing Overview

  • Pay as you go based on the usage of the AI assistant.
  • Message Fee: ~0.03€ per message sent by the AI assistant.
  • MOM AI Order Fee: 0.10€ + 1% of the total price of each successfully paid order.
  • PayPal Order Fee: 0.35€ + 3.49% of the total price of each successfully paid order.
  • Opt for adding MOM AI and PayPal fees to be paid by the customer, turning it into an astonishing 0% cost for you to use MOM AI service.
  • No upfront payments required! At the start, you receive 3€ to cover the AI-usage and generate your first AI order!

How do I access my funds?

Once you earn 100€ on the MOM AI Restaurant platform, visit the "Withdraw Funds" section on your dashboard and request the withdrawal of your funds in a way convenient for you!
